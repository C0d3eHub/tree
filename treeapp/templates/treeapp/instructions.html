Navigation Guide

  • Use the top menu to access Tree, Gallery, Posts, Blood Bank, and more.
  • On the Tree page, drag to pan and use your mouse wheel or pinch to zoom.
  • Click on a family member to view details or right-click to focus on a branch.
  • On mobile, tap the menu icon (|||) to open navigation.

Editing Guide

  • Log in and go to My Tree from the menu.
  • Click the info icon next to your name, then select Edit Member.
  • Update your details and click Save. Changes may require admin approval.
  • Admins can approve, reject, or unpublish content from the admin dashboard.

Mobile Use Guide

  • The site is fully responsive. Cards and menus stack vertically on mobile.
  • Use the emoji menu for quick access to features.
  • Tap outside open menus or modals to close them.
  • Pinch to zoom and drag to pan the family tree on touch devices.
