Instructions

How to Navigate the Family Tree

Follow these steps to effectively navigate through the family tree:

  • Click on 🌲 Tree in the navigation menu to view the complete family tree.
  • Use your mouse to pan around the tree by clicking and dragging.
  • Zoom in and out using the mouse wheel or pinch gestures on touch devices.
  • Click on any family member to view their details.
  • Right-click on a member to track their specific branch of the family tree.

Updating Your Information

To update your personal information in the family tree:

  1. Log in to your account.
  2. Navigate to 👪 My Tree in the menu.
  3. Click on the ℹ️ info icon next to your name.
  4. Select Edit Member from the options.
  5. Update your information including name, birth year, and photo.
  6. Click Save to apply your changes.

Note: Your updates may require admin approval before they appear in the main tree.

Submitting Corrections

If you notice incorrect information in the family tree:

  1. Click on the name of the family member with incorrect information.
  2. Select the "Submit Correction" option.
  3. Fill out the correction form with the accurate information.
  4. Submit your correction for review.

An administrator will review your submission and make the necessary changes if approved.

Adding New Family Members

To request adding a new family member to the tree:

  1. Navigate to the family tree view.
  2. Click on the parent to whom you want to add a child.
  3. Select "Add Member" from the options.
  4. Fill out the form with the new member's information.
  5. Submit your request for admin approval.

Note: Only authenticated users can submit requests to add new members.
