Welcome to the Family Tree App
वंशावली सारस्वत वशिष्ट गौत्र बांजणी परिवार
This application is dedicated to preserving and exploring the rich ancestry of the Vashisht village (Banjani) families.

Browse your family tree, discover your roots, and contribute to the collective knowledge of our community.

Features:
  • Click on menu → Tree to view family tree
  • Interactive Vashisht family tree visualization
  • Add, correct, and compare family member records
  • Preserve the stories and history of the Banjani community
  • Right Click to Track Your Family Tree
  • Compare Generations
  • Add Name Request
  • Correction Name
  • Click Name to send correction name
Note: This digital family tree is a living document. All Banjani families are welcome to participate and help ensure our heritage is never lost.
